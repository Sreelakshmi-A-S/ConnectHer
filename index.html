About Us

The importance of women connecting with each other cannot be overstated. Women connect in various ways, whether it's through friendships, communities, or online platforms. Here are some reasons why women connect and why it holds significant value: Empowerment and Support: Women connect to empower and support each other. By sharing their experiences, challenges, and successes, women can inspire and uplift one another. Connecting with like-minded individuals who understand and empathize with their struggles can provide a sense of validation, encouragement, and motivation. Solidarity and Unity: Connecting with other women fosters a sense of solidarity and unity. It creates a space where women can come together, united by shared experiences and a common goal. Women connect to build a supportive network, celebrate achievements, and advocate for their rights and interests collectively. Exchange of Knowledge and Advice: Women connect to seek advice, exchange knowledge, and learn from each other. It's a platform for sharing expertise, insights, and wisdom gained from personal experiences. This exchange of information can be instrumental in personal growth, professional development, and decision-making. Overcoming Isolation and Loneliness: Women can often face isolation and loneliness due to various factors such as societal expectations, cultural barriers, or personal circumstances. Connecting with other women helps combat these feelings of isolation, providing a sense of belonging and a community of individuals who can relate to and support each other. Mentorship and Role Modeling: Women connect to establish mentorship relationships and find role models who inspire them. Mentorship offers guidance, advice, and opportunities for personal and professional development. Having access to successful and supportive role models can significantly impact a woman's confidence and career trajectory. Collective Action and Social Change: Women connecting and organizing collectively have historically played a vital role in driving social change and advocating for women's rights. By coming together, women can amplify their voices, challenge gender norms, and work towards achieving equality and empowerment for all women. Personal Growth and Self-Discovery: Connecting with other women provides an opportunity for personal growth and self-discovery. It allows individuals to explore their identities, values, and aspirations in a supportive environment. Through meaningful connections, women can gain a deeper understanding of themselves and their place in the world. Thus women connect to find strength, support, and empowerment in a world that sometimes underestimates or undervalues their experiences and contributions. It is through these connections that women can amplify their voices, find solidarity, and create positive change for themselves and future generations.

More About Us

Women In Technology (WIT) empowers girls and women to excel in science, technology, engineering, the arts, and math (STEAM) from the classroom to the boardroom. We do this by providing female students in middle school, high school, and college with education, exposure and experience. By sharing the stories of successful professionals across the STEAM fields, we encourage all generations of girls and women to write their future. Today, WIT has over 20,000 members in Georgia, 50,000 members worldwide and our team of more than 250 volunteers deliver professional development and networking opportunities to each of our professionals and programs at no charge to more than 3,000 students. WIT is a non-profit organization and we are committed to making Georgia the state with the highest percentage of women in the STEAM workforce by partnering with our community to provide opportunities that champion women throughout their education and career.
